Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

1. Who we are

FinchMart QBO Connector ("the Application") is a private, internal-use integration operated by FinchMart to record its own business expenses in its own QuickBooks Online company file.

2. What data we access

Through the Intuit API, the Application accesses only the operator's own QuickBooks Online company data: expense (purchase) transactions, vendors, chart of accounts, and file attachments (receipt images) that we create.

3. What data we collect and store

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored locally on the operator's own computer, protected by file-system permissions. Receipt images originate from the operator's own business purchases. No customer or third-party personal data is collected.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Data flows only between the operator's computer and Intuit's QuickBooks Online service over HTTPS.

5. Data retention and deletion

Tokens may be deleted at any time by the operator, and access can be revoked from the QuickBooks Online connected-apps settings, which invalidates all tokens.

6. Security

All API communication uses TLS (HTTPS). Credentials and tokens are excluded from version control and stored with restrictive file permissions.
